The Meatless Burger

The history

We enjoy meat. We like meat, we love meat, we always want some more of it. However, this was always a contentious problem when barbecues inevitably came on summers. Some of our friends are vegetarians or vegans. Not eating meat at a barbecue means they were not getting the 100% barbecue experience. And we decided to fix it! The meatless burger was born.

We started digging into the meat that the burger patties we all know and love are made of. Turns out that, whatever burger patties are made of, can be easily made thanks to the fancy lab equipment that we have available! Easy stuff. With a model on what the burger should contain, we made the lab boys walk backwards from that point. And so they did! After throwing a bunch of ingredients to the wall and see what the hell sticks, the labs boys did figure it out. Using ingredients similar to the chemical concoction out of our lab, we finally we were able to replicate pretty darn well a burger patty, with 100% less animal products.

And boy, people liked it
